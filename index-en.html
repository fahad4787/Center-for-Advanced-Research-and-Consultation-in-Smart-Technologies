Vision

Leadership in advanced research and studies and innovation in artificial intelligence and data analytics, providing specialized scientific consultations with strategic impact.

Mission

Produce advanced knowledge in artificial intelligence and data analytics, develop strategic research and studies, and provide specialized scientific consultations.

General Objectives

  • Produce advanced research and studies in artificial intelligence, data analytics, and related technologies.
  • Support innovation, research, and development in emerging smart technologies.
  • Provide scientific, methodological, and specialized consultations in smart technologies.
  • Contribute to building effective policies and governance frameworks for the use of artificial intelligence and data.
  • Build national and international strategic partnerships in research and development.
