Introduction and Center Entity Definition

The proposed entity is the "Center for Advanced Research and Consultation in Smart Technologies," aimed at being a specialized scientific and advisory platform dedicated to producing advanced knowledge, developing strategic studies, and providing trusted scientific consultation in artificial intelligence, data analytics, and related technologies. The center also aims to support public and private entities with research and advisory outputs built on scientific and methodological foundations that contribute to effective and accountable use of smart technologies, enhancing innovation, and raising institutional readiness.

Vision

Leadership in advanced research and studies and innovation in artificial intelligence and data analytics, and providing specialized scientific consultation with strategic impact.

Mission

Producing advanced knowledge in artificial intelligence and data analytics, developing strategic research and studies, and providing specialized scientific consultation.

General Objectives

  • Producing advanced research and studies in artificial intelligence, data analytics, and related technologies.
  • Supporting innovation, research, and development in emerging smart technologies.
  • Providing scientific, methodological, and specialized consultation in smart technologies.
  • Contributing to building effective governance policies and frameworks for the use of artificial intelligence and data.
  • Building national and international strategic partnerships in research and development.

Scope of Work

Includes conducting advanced research and studies, preparing strategic analyses and reports, providing scientific consultation, implementing research and foresight initiatives, and building academic and institutional partnerships in artificial intelligence, data analytics, data science, and related smart technologies.
